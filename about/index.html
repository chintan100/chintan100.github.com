About Chintan

I am an iOS Developer with 4+ years of full time work experience with iOS SDK. I began developing iOS apps in January 2009 and quit my full-time job in November 2012 to become an independent iOS Developer.

Here is a list of iOS apps i have developed:

  • Anthm/Jukio : An app whose mission is to establish musical democracy by crowdsourcing your party playlist. Featured on TechCrunch, BBC radio etc.

  • Sears Catalogues : The famous Sears Catalogue now on your iPad. App is only available in Canadian App Store.

  • TouchPoint - Business : A B2B application for companies/organizations/institutions to collect feedback from their customers/employees. Clients include some Fortune 500 companies like Amazon, Accenture, Bank of America and government institutions like US Army, US Navy etc. Complete client list.

  • Vexx SMS : A WhatsApp-like cross-platform voice and text messaging client developed in South Africa with a mission to eliminate the need of using traditional SMS.

  • Foursight : An “easy to learn, hard to master” boardgame with an original concept. Can be played against computer or another player.

  • Talking Puppy & Talking Santa: Fun apps which mimic what you say in a funny tone.

  • LoveArt : A love postcards app with uniquely designed original postcards to express your love for someone. More than 100,000 downloads. Ranked #1 overall free app in Spain, #2 in France and Brazil.

  • NYC Guide : A handy guide to little known shops/markets in NYC worth checking out.

  • Property Register : Developed to provide an easy way for real-estate clients to keep track of various property listings and to collect contact information/feedback from visitors in a property show.

  • Wifi HD Free Wireless Hard Disk : Turn your iOS Device into a wireless pen drive. Transfer files to/from your iPhone wirelessly over WiFi. Once ranked in Top 20 free business apps in US App Store.

  • Motivator : People wanting to quit smoking can take help of this app to keep track of their progress.

  • Pokebase : A database of Pokemons for easy reference.

  • Property Investment Calculator : An easy way to calculate property related costs/rents/prices etc.

  • scratcher : Add photos of cars and scratch them with your favorite objects.

  • AdventAlarm : A christmas themed alarm with countdown to christmas.

  • WhoLovesMe : A simple game to find out who loves you from amongst your contacts.

  • VoiceNotes Professional : App converts dictated speech into text using Google Speech API. The text can then be copied, emailed, tweeted, posted on facebook etc. from within the app.

  • Centurion 100 Club : Use this app to keep track of your drinking game on your way to hit a centurion.
